About Unicode Cleaner

Unicode Cleaner is a free online tool designed to detect and remove hidden Unicode characters such as zero-width spaces (U+200B), non-breaking spaces (U+00A0), byte order marks (U+FEFF), and other invisible formatting characters.

These hidden characters often appear when copying text from AI tools, PDFs, Microsoft Word, or websites. While invisible, they can break JSON files, cause syntax errors in code, disrupt database entries, and trigger unexpected validation failures.

Why This Tool Exists

Developers, students, and content creators frequently encounter frustrating copy-paste issues. Unicode Cleaner was created to provide a fast, private, and browser-based solution to instantly detect and remove problematic characters.

Privacy First

Unicode Cleaner runs entirely in your browser. No text is uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server. Everything happens locally on your device.

Who Is It For?

  • Developers debugging JSON or code errors
  • Students submitting assignments
  • Writers copying from AI tools
  • Anyone facing invisible character issues

Our goal is to keep the tool simple, fast, and secure for everyone.
